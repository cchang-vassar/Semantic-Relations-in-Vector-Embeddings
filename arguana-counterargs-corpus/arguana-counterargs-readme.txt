--------------------------
ARGUANA COUNTERARGS CORPUS
--------------------------

The corpus consists of three parts:
- 01-crawled-webpages- 02-extracted-arguments- 03-pairs-best-counter-task

The first one contains the crawled idebate.org web pages, organized according to the datasets and themes.

The second one contains all extracted debate titles, introductory texts, pointers, counters, bibliographies, and metadata, organized according to the datasets, themes, and debates.

The third one contains the experiment files that store the true and false pairs, organized according to the datasets and themes.

Details are found in the following paper, which is published at the ACL 2018:

@inproceedings{wachsmuth:2018a,
  	author = 	"Wachsmuth, Henning and Syed, Shahbaz and Stein, Benno",
